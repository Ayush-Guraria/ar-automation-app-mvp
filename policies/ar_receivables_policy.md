# Accounts Receivable Payment Terms & Collections Policy

**Classification: Internal Use Only**
**Document Owner: Accounts Receivable / Collections**

## Payment Terms

Payment terms are net 30 days from invoice date.

## Reminders

Invoices unpaid beyond 30 days will receive a courtesy reminder.

## Late Fees

Invoices unpaid beyond 60 days are subject to a 1.5% monthly late fee.

## Escalation

Invoices unpaid beyond 90 days are escalated to senior management and flagged
for collections review.

## VP Approval Threshold

Clients with outstanding balances over $25,000 beyond 60 days require VP
approval before collections referral.

## Payment Disputes

All payment disputes must be submitted in writing within 15 days of invoice
receipt.

## Partial Payments

Partial payments are accepted but do not reset the overdue clock.

## Early Payment Discount

Early payment discount of 2% is available for invoices paid within 10 days.
